About

United College of Engineering and Research (UCER) is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education in engineering and technology. Established with the vision of fostering innovation and excellence, UCER offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, a well-stocked library, and recreational areas to ensure a conducive learning environment. The college boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are committed to nurturing the talents and skills of our students. At UCER, we emphasize practical learning through industry collaborations, internships, workshops, and seminars. Our curriculum is designed to keep pace with the latest technological advancements and industry trends, preparing our students for successful careers in their chosen fields. Beyond academics, UCER encourages students to participate in extracurricular activities, cultural events, and sports to promote holistic development. We believe in shaping well-rounded individuals who can contribute positively to society. Join us at United College of Engineering and Research, where innovation meets excellence, and dreams turn into reality.

Department

At United College of Engineering and Research, we offer a wide range of engineering and professional programs designed to build strong technical expertise and practical problem-solving skills. Each department is guided by highly qualified faculty, modern labs, and an innovative curriculum to ensure the best learning experience.

The Department Of Computer Science And Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology is a lively and invigorating academic center for higher education, research, and innovation in key areas of computing. The vision of the department is to be a center of excellence that provides high-quality education to develop future leaders in all aspects of computing, including education and entrepreneurship, contributes practically relevant and cutting-edge research, and catalyzes IT entrepreneurship. The department attracts talented students and faculty from all over India. The main Endeavour of the department is to develop students’ technical competence as well as creative and critical thinking ability. The department provides the students with a platform to develop insight to face real-life challenges and to come out with innovative technical solutions. They are frequently evaluated with multiple assignments, hands-on exercises, mini projects, surprise tests and presentations as they progress through the different programs in the department. The department uses a diversified set of pedagogical approaches that includes interactive lectures and practical hands-on exercises, etc. The team has dedicated and well-qualified faculty members to facilitate a high standard of teaching & learning process. These faculty members are also involved in research work, attaining research publications in repute international conferences and journals.

Information Technology (IT)

Dedicated to IT solutions, networking, cybersecurity, and database management. Students learn to design and manage advanced information systems for the digital world.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Blending electronics, communication, and embedded systems to prepare students for careers in telecom, robotics, IoT, and semiconductor industries

Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Building expertise in power systems, renewable energy, and control systems, enabling students to meet the challenges of the ever-growing energy sector.
